Photogram

An AR experience for memory exploration and photography.

Problem

How do friends communicate?

Group messaging. You have the freedom to communicate what you want, when you want, and to multiple people at once. Group messages, however, are an archive of thoughts, and are not optimized for finding a mutual time to get together.

Solution

Events for iMessage allows friends to find mutually available times and organize integrated events all from iMessage.


Start the conversation.

Checking availability and planning events has always been a conversation. From the simple "I miss you, let's hangout" text to sharing availabilities and creating a Calendar linked event, Events for iMessage makes this conversation digital.

AR Log Line

"A participant puts on the Hololens and has the ability to capture a memory in a 3D space (a “Photogram”), where they are able to play back their own past Photograms, connect with the Photograms of others, and explore the Photograms of that location. The experience goal is to redefine memory retention, recall, and development while capitalizing on empathy."
