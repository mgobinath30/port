Potter Pics

A Harry Potter themed photo-sharing social network.

Overview

Potter Pics is a fun experiment that allowed me to explore two of my favorite worlds: Harry Potter and iOS.

Simply enter the app with The Unlocking Charm, get sorted into a random house, and start posting photos for the rest of the magical world.

Process

There are endless aspects of the Harry Potter world that could translate to a digital, social experience. I experimented with sorting house quizzes, interactive maps of Hogwarts, using spells to unlock features.

Ultimately, I decided that simple, visual references to Harry Potter are more compelling than concepts that may exist in the wizarding world, but make no sense for a social app.
