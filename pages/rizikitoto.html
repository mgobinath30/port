Rizikitoto

Help East African orphans become self-sufficient. Visit rizikitoto.org to learn more.

Overview

Riziki, meaning sustenance & toto, meaning child.

Rizikitoto works to help East African orphans become self-sufficient. We’ve built a network of mobile apps where the revenue of each app is spent on resources that support the kids’ talents, interests, and aspirations.
